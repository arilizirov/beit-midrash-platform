# DAMA-DMBOK — notes (original summary)

> The kit's own paraphrase of this book's key principles, to guide judgment.
> This is NOT the book's text. For depth, read the book itself.

The DAMA Data Management Body of Knowledge: data as a managed enterprise asset.

- Governance, quality, metadata, security, and master/reference data are distinct, named disciplines.
- Quality must be designed, measured, monitored, and corrected — storage alone guarantees nothing.
- Ownership and stewardship are organisational, not just technical.
